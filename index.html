About

Klypp LLC builds secure remote-to-home media workflow software. Our flagship product, Klypp, connects your mobile devices to your home media server through a secure relay, enabling private, authenticated transfers and management.

Privacy

We design our products to minimize data collection. Klypp processes transfers end-to-end with authentication and encryption. We do not sell personal data.

For privacy inquiries, visit Support.

Products

Klypp — a secure relay-backed app that moves files from your phone to your home server.

  • Platform: iOS & Android (in development)
  • Server: .NET 8 / Linux & Windows
  • Relay: zero-knowledge secure relay with mutual TLS (mTLS); traffic is end-to-end encrypted and never stored in the cloud
  • Deployment: runs on your own hardware/NAS or VPS; no third-party accounts required
