About

Klypp LLC builds secure remote-to-home media workflow software. Our flagship product, Klypp, connects your mobile devices to your home media server through a secure relay, enabling private, authenticated transfers and management for media you own or are licensed to use.

This is the official website for Klypp LLC, publicly available at klyppedit.com.

Products

Klypp — a secure relay-backed app that moves files from your phone to your self-hosted media server. Features include certificate-based auth, end-to-end encrypted transport, and a focus on personal, authorized libraries (no third-party catalogs or downloads).

  • Platform: iOS & Android (in development)
  • Server: .NET 8 · runs on Linux, macOS, Windows, Raspberry Pi, or NAS (self-hosted)
  • Relay: managed by Klypp LLC with mutual TLS (mTLS); traffic is transient and never stored

Privacy

We design our products to minimize data collection. Klypp processes transfers with authentication and encryption. We do not sell personal data.
